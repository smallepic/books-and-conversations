Books & Conversations

Books & Conversations is an ongoing dialogue, born out of a desire to combine two great loves—thought-provoking books and interesting conversation.

It’s essentially a book club—pick a book, invite an eclectic mix of friends to read it; and host a conversation to share, debate, and educate.
